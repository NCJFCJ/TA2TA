Virtual Services

Virtual Services

Engage many participants at once by taking advantage of the webinar service available through the TA2TA Resource Center. TA providers may host a webinar of up to 1,000 participants at no cost. Once your presentation and presenters have been determined, TA2TA will set up your webinar, assist you in hosting it, conduct trial runs for presenters, and secure closed captioning. At the conclusion of your webinar, you will receive a webinar report with participant numbers, a recording of your webinar, and the closed captioning transcript.

Roundtable Services

Roundtable Services

TA providers have the opportunity to convene roundtables to delve deeper into an issue and to identify emerging or complex issues that will advance and guide the fields of domestic violence, sexual assault, dating violence, and stalking. The TA2TA Resource Center may cover the cost of hosting virtual or in-person roundtables, in whole or in part, as determined by OVW.

Newsletter

Newsletter

The TA2TA Resource Center newsletter is distributed every other month. This newsletter includes updates on new TA2TA website features, highlights TA2TA services, and provides guidance on grant management challenges and opportunities. All TA providers with a registered website account and OVW receive the newsletter. To submit a highlight or to suggest a topic for inclusion in the newsletter, please email info@TA2TA.org. You can control your newsletter preferences through your user profile after signing in.

Peer to Peer Collaborations

Peer to Peer Collaborations

The TA2TA facilitates peer-to-peer collaborations specifically for TA providers to connect with fellow TA providers. Such collaborations include TA2TA connect calls, TA2TA office hours, and topic-specific dicussions and meetings identified by TA providers.
