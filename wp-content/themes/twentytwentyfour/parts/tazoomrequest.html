Screen-sharing
  • In Zoom Meetings, this is how to display a document (PDF/Word Doc/PowerPoint) for a presentation-based meeting.
  • Similarly, this can be used to show videos as well as to show participants how to navigate websites by watching the screenshare.
Polling
  • This function allows for in-meeting polling of participants.
  • Only single-selection, multiple choice polling is available.
Attendees
  • Muted and No Video.
Chat Function
  • Allow participants to send chats to everyone or just panelists.
  • Chat can be disabled as well.
Question and Answer Box
  • Allow participants to submit questions to panelists and be viewed by all or just panelists. Panelists can then respond verbally, private message, or public message to display both the question and answer to all attendees.

Breakout rooms
  • This breaks participants up into smaller collaborative groups for discussions.
  • Participants can be automatically or manually be sorted by the TA2TA Host.
  • Note: Closed captioning and interpretation is not available in breakout rooms.
Whiteboard
  • A blank white board for all participants to annotate using text, stamps, shapes, drawings, etc....
On-screen Annotation
  • Have a chart you want participants to engage in? Build this into your word document, PDF or PowerPoint presentation and participants can annotate directly on the screen.
File sharing
  • Share files (Word Document/PDF/PowerPoint) from your computer, Google Drive, or iCloud in the chat.
Chat Function
  • Allow participants to send chats to everyone.
  • Allow participants to send private chats to other participants.
